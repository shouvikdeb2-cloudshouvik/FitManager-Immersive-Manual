2 Activation & TrialUnlock FitManager permanently with your licence key
2.1 The Trial Period
▲ A red bar at the top counts down your 30-day free trial. All features are available during this time.
FitManager includes a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. When the trial expires, the
software switches to read-only mode — you can still view your data but cannot add members or
record payments.
2.2 How to Activate
▲ Activation popup — copy your Machine ID, send it to the developer, then paste your licence key.
1
Open the activation popup
Click "Activate Now" in the red trial bar, or wait for the popup to appear after trial expiry.
2
Copy your Machine ID
Click on the code shown in the popup, then press Ctrl+A and Ctrl+C to copy it.
3
Contact the developer
Send your Machine ID to Shouvik Deb. You will receive a unique licence key tied to your
computer.
4
Paste the licence key
Paste the key into the "Paste License Key Here" field and click Activate.
5
Restart FitManager
Close and reopen the app. The red trial bar will be gone permanently.

8.4 Backups — Your Most Important Task
FitManager stores all your data locally on your computer. If your hard drive fails and you have no
backup, your data is gone. Back up regularly.
Creating a Backup
20. Go to Admin → Backup & Reset.
21. Click "Backup Data". A .json file downloads to your computer.
22. Move this file to a USB drive, Google Drive, or OneDrive.
Restoring from Backup
23. Go to Admin → Backup & Reset.
24. Click "Import Data".
25. Select your saved .json backup file. All data is restored.
Gym Size Recommended Backup Frequency
```
Small (under 50 members) Once per week
```
```
Medium (50–200 members) Every 2–3 days
```
```
Large (200+ members) Every day
```

🐀
```
Warning: NEVER click "Reset System" unless you want to permanently delete ALL your
```
data. This action cannot be undone.

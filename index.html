SECTION 01

Getting Started

Install FitManager and log in for the first time. Follow these quick steps to perform the first-time setup efficiently.

System Requirements

Windows 10+
2GB RAM
200MB Space
No Internet Required

1. Activate the Software: Enter the master key provided with your software to unlock the first-time setup.

2. Gym Branding: Enter your gym name and optionally upload a logo. Both appear on every page and on printed receipts.

3. Create Admin Account: Set your admin username and password. If you forget your admin password, use your provided emergency key.

4. First Login: Leave Username blank for admin, enter your password, then click Login to launch the Dashboard.

SECTION 02

Activation & Trial

Unlock FitManager permanently with your licence key. Activate before your 30-day trial ends to avoid interruptions.

The Trial Period: A red bar at the top counts down your 30-day free trial. All features are available during this time.

How to Activate: Copy your Machine ID, send it to the developer, and paste the unique licence key you receive to unlock permanently.

SECTION 03

Dashboard

Your gym's command centre — open this every morning for an instant health check of your gym's stats.

Summary Cards: Instantly view Total Members (Blue), Active Members (Green), Pending Members (Amber), and Blocked Members (Red).

Revenue Summary & Date Filters: View Today's Collection, Monthly Revenue, and Recent Activities using time filters.

SECTION 04

Managing Members

Add, view, edit, block, freeze, and search members. Visual badges tell you their current payment status at a glance.

The Members List: Every gym member appears here. Search by name, apply status/date filters, and use action buttons to manage them.

Adding a New Member: Click "+ Add Member", fill in required fields (Name, Phone, Plan), and they are added with Pending status.

Viewing a Member Profile: Click the eye icon to see their status badge, next bill date, total amount paid, and more details.

Editing a Member: Click the pencil icon to update any field. Note: Staff accounts may require Admin permission to edit.

Profile Tab - Payments: Full payment history for this member with date, amount, method, and the staff member who processed it.

Profile Tab - Freeze History: Temporarily pause memberships (no billing/reminders). See past freeze periods and their statuses.

Profile Tab - Notes: Write and save private staff notes or special arrangements securely regarding this specific member.

SECTION 05

Recording Payments

Log every payment immediately to keep balances accurate and member statuses updated.

The Payment Form: Click the card icon next to a member to open the form. Complete with Amount, Months to Pay, and Payment Method.

The Payments Page: View a full list of every transaction recorded across all members, alongside total collection summaries.

SECTION 06

Sending Reminders

WhatsApp payment reminders for members who owe money. Send individually or all at once.

The Message Template: Customise the template using {NAME} and {AMOUNT}. Click the green WhatsApp button to send, or "Bulk WhatsApp" for everyone.

SECTION 07

Reports

Charts and analytics to understand your gym's performance securely from your local database.

Key Metrics & Report Tabs: View Total Revenue, Average Monthly Revenue, Collection Rates, and use specific date filters. Export all as a PDF.

SECTIONS 08 & 09

Admin & Quick Reference

Configure settings, manage staff, protect your data, and use daily checklists to stay on track.

Admin & Backups

Membership Plans: Set default prices in Admin > Membership Plans (e.g., Basic at 1,500/mo).

Auto-Block Rules: Create rules to block members automatically after X unpaid months, with grace periods.

Staff Accounts: Assign roles ("Staff" for limited access, "Admin" for full access).

Backups: Your most important task. Locally store database via Admin > Backup & Reset. Save your JSON file manually to a USB or Cloud Drive.

Daily Checklist

Morning: Open FitManager, check Dashboard. Note pending/blocked members.

During Day: Add new members, record received payments immediately.

Evening: Check Today's Collection on Dashboard. Send WhatsApp Reminders. Perform Admin Backup and close the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it need internet?

No. FitManager works 100% offline after installation. Your data never leaves your computer.

Can I use on multiple PCs?

Each computer needs its own license. The key is tied to your specific hardware.

What does "Pending" mean?

Member has unpaid months. Check the "Due Amount" column to see how much they owe.

What is "Frozen" status?

Membership is paused. No billing or reminders during freeze (travel, injury, etc.).

Can members pay in advance?

Yes! Enter multiple months in the "Months Paid" field when recording payment.

How to move data to new PC?

Backup on old PC → Install on new PC → Get new license → Import backup file.
